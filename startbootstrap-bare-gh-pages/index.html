About Me

Hi!
I'm a full stack web developer fresh into the wonderful world of coding and looking for full-time opportunities in order to immerse myself into the tech industry.


After having worked in the company secretarial field for four years, I realized the corporate life wasn't for me. So in January 2019 I took the plunge to quit my job and in March 2019, I joined the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at NEXT Academy in order to make a massive career change. I realized very quickly after joining that coding was what I wanted to be able to do for the rest of my life.


I am a self-driven, hardworking individual who is motivated to continuously learn and improve.

Projects

Helikopter

For our final group project at NEXT Academy on the theme of Indie Projects/1K Projects, we built both a Google Chrome extension and an interactive web-app, aimed towards protecting young children from potentially sensitive, graphic and violent content on the internet by censoring keywords, blurring images and disabling links associated with said keywords


Helikopter won two out of three categories, Best Pitch and Best App, during the NEXT Academy Pitch Night, whereby we showcased our app to mentors, students and web developers.


Sandygram

An interactive Instagram clone built during the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at NEXT Academy. The back-end server was built using Python, PostgreSQL and Peewee and the front-end with Jinja and Bootstrap.


Features that were implemented include AWS S3 to store images, Sendgrid to handle email notifications, Google OAuth and Braintree to handle donations.

Nextagram

An interactive Instagram clone built with React during the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at NEXT Academy.
